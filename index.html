Test your understanding of physics concepts with these interactive problems!

Question 1: A car accelerates from 0 to 30 m/s in 6 seconds. What is its acceleration?
  • A) 3 m/s¬≤
  • B) 5 m/s¬≤
  • C) 6 m/s¬≤
  • D) 30 m/s¬≤
Correct! Using v = u + at: 30 = 0 + a(6), so a = 5 m/s¬≤
Question 2: According to Newton's second law, if the net force on an object doubles while mass stays constant, the acceleration:
  • A) Stays the same
  • B) Doubles
  • C) Becomes half
  • D) Becomes zero
Correct! F = ma, so if F doubles and m is constant, then a must double too.
Question 3: A 2 kg ball is dropped from 20 m height. What is its kinetic energy just before hitting the ground? (g = 10 m/s¬≤)
  • A) 200 J
  • B) 400 J
  • C) 40 J
  • D) 800 J
Correct! By conservation of energy: PE = KE = mgh = 2 √ó 10 √ó 20 = 400 J
