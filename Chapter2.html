üè≠ Material Categories

üî© Brittle Materials

Examples: Glass, ceramics, cast iron

Characteristics:

  • Little plastic deformation before failure
  • High compressive strength
  • Low tensile strength
  • Sudden failure without warning

üîß Ductile Materials

Examples: Steel, copper, aluminum

Characteristics:

  • Significant plastic deformation
  • Can be drawn into wires
  • Give warning before failure
  • High toughness

üå∏ Elastic Materials

Examples: Rubber, springs, tendons

Characteristics:

  • Return to original shape after deformation
  • Large elastic deformation range
  • Store and release energy efficiently
  • Non-linear stress-strain relationship
